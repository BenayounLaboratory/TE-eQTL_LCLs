README -- GWAS Analysis of L1 trans-eQTLs
############################

  1. Collect details for L1 trans-eQTLs in one file to provide to collaborators  (Prepare_Files_for_Collaborators.R)

  2. Visualize results provided by Suh lab collaborators (Plot_GWAS_Results.R)

  3. Generate random combinations of SNVs, map L1 eQTLs and random SNVs to Open Targets, and assess enrichment of L1 eQTLs for disease categories relative to random SNVs (Open_Targets_Trait_Category_Enrichment.R, Open_Targets_Trait_Category_Enrichment_functions.R).
